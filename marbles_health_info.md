# Marbles Health - Company Overview

## General Information
- **Name:** Marbles Health
- **Founded:** 2020
- **Founders:** Lakshay Sahni and Ramya Yellapragada
- **Location:** India
- **Mission/Focus:** Brain healthcare company redefining standards of care across psychiatry and neurology using non-invasive neuromodulation.

## Flagship Product: EASE
- **What it is:** India's first medically licensed, portable tDCS (Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation) device.
- **How it works:** Uses mild electric currents to stimulate specific areas of the brain. It is designed as a scalable, medication-free alternative or supplement to traditional psychiatric treatments.
- **Platform/App:** The EASE hardware pairs via Bluetooth with the "Ease" app. The app provides guided neuromodulation therapy, Cognitive Emotional Training (CET), visualization exercises, session management, and progress tracking.
- **Care Model:** Emphasizes a clinical orientation, combining the tDCS device, CET, and therapist/clinician oversight. 

## Clinical Applications & Approvals
- **Regulatory Status:** CDSCO (India's FDA) approved.
- **Adoption:** Used in hospitals and clinics across India.
- **Conditions Treated / Target Areas:**
  - Depression and Anxiety
  - Addiction
  - ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder)
  - OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder)
  - Aphasia
  - Cognitive decline

- **Target Audience:** The content should move away from a generic "healthcare startup" tone. Instead, it should sound like it is coming from innovators and creators of a tangible, cutting-edge medical device—speaking directly to users and clinicians with confidence and clarity.
- **Focus Points:** Portability, safety (medication-free), clinical validation (CDSCO approved), and a comprehensive care model (hardware + software + therapist).
