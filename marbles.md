Marbles Health — Research Summary
Company: Operates as Neuracle Health Private Limited. Product is called EASE. Site built on Framer, "Tailor-made by Tetrix" (their dev agency credit in the footer).
Core positioning
EASE is marketed as "India's first and only CDSCO-approved (Indian FDA) neuromodulation device" for neuro-psychiatric conditions, offering "Breakthrough Neuro Psychiatry Care — With or Without Medication."
What EASE actually is — a 3-layer system in one wearable headband

tDCS (Transcranial Direct Current Stimulation) — gentle electrical brain stimulation targeting the prefrontal cortex (F3/F4, dlPFC), -2 to +2mA, 20V protection limit, 0.01mA resolution, overcurrent/voltage protection.
4-channel EEG — hospital-grade, dry electrodes, 250/500Hz sampling, zero prep, continuous impedance checks, tracks brainwave bands (calm-alpha to focused-beta).
CET (Cognitive Emotional Training) — adaptive in-app exercises for memory, attention, emotional regulation.
