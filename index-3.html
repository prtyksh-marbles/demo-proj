What is EASE?

India's first
CDSCO-approved
neuromodulation device.

Three technologies. One connected device. EASE combines gentle brain stimulation, real-time brain activity monitoring, and guided cognitive exercises into one connected device. Designed for clinical use, it helps deliver personalized brain care with precision and confidence.

Built as one system, not three add-ons. Every EASE session runs all three technologies together, stimulation, sensing, and training, working in sync rather than as separate tools bolted onto a headband.

tDCS: Wakes up the circuits that fall quiet. Gentle electrical stimulation targets the brain regions that regulate mood and focus, nudging them back into balance, with nothing invasive and no surgery involved.

EEG: Listens to the brain as it changes. Real-time sensors track brain activity thousands of times a second, so every session adjusts to what's actually happening in your head, not a fixed routine.

CET: Trains the mind while it heals. Adaptive cognitive and emotional exercises sharpen memory, attention, and emotional control, getting smarter about you with every session.

Certified where it counts. EASE is CDSCO-approved and backed by 8+ ISO/IEC certifications, with safety and efficacy validated across multiple independent clinical trials.
